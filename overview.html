WATER AND SANITAION

What is Clean water and Sanitation?

Clean water refers to water that is safe, accessible and free from harmful substances such as pollutants, bacteria and chemicals.

Sanitation is the provision of facilities and services for the disposal of human wastes as well as the maintenance of hygiene conditions through clean environments,hand washing facilities and proper waste management.

Good sanitation prevents the spread of diseases and ensures dignity, health, and well-being.
